About Us

As a dedicated mentor, I am passionate about supporting the next generation of professionals in pharmaceutical and clinical research.

Through my involvement with the Black Pharma mentoring program and as a member of Black Women in Clinical Research, I bring both industry expertise and a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by underrepresented groups in our field.

I have had the privilege of guiding numerous science students as they navigate their transition into the pharmaceutical industry, helping them understand career pathways, develop essential skills, and build the confidence needed to succeed.

My mentoring approach combines practical industry insights with personalized guidance, drawing from my own professional journey to help mentees overcome obstacles and identify opportunities that align with their goals and aspirations.
